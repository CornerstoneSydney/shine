Captain Responsibilities

Before the Event

  • Team Recruitment: Use the sign-up link/QR code to recruit a full team from your congregation
  • Gender Requirement: Ensure at least one female player is registered and will be present
  • Communication: Be active in the Captains' WhatsApp group for updates
  • Team Preparation: Inform your team about:
    • Game times and location
    • Team colors/jersey requirements
    • Tournament rules and format
  • Special Responsibility (Derrick Mar - Eastwood): Coordinate with Paul Gray to organise the pickup, setup, and return of goal posts and field-marking cones

On Game Day

  • 1:00 PM: Attend the mandatory Team Captains briefing
  • Team Management:
    • Ensure your team arrives promptly before each match
    • Organise team drinks/snacks (or remind them to bring their own)
    • Manage substitutions during games
    • Ensure your team is at the right field at the right time
    • Communicate with the referee regarding any issues
    • Bring a Soccer Ball per congregation
  • Sportsmanship: Uphold and encourage good sportsmanship among your team
  • Safety: Report any injuries to the First Aid tent immediately

Tournament Rules & Guidelines

üèÉ Game Format

  • Team Size: 7-a-side (6 field players + 1 goalkeeper)
  • Gender Rule: Minimum 1 female player on the field at ALL times
  • Substitutions: Unlimited rolling substitutions (subs can be made during play)
  • Game Duration: 15 minutes each way; 5-minute halftime; 5-minute changeover between games
  • Match Ball: Size 5 ball (please bring a ball per congregation)
  • Fields: 3 fields will be used, each with a size of 60m x 40m
  • Goal Size: 3m x 2m goals
  • Match Officials: 1 referee per game

Key Rules

  • Offside: No offside rule
  • Goal Kicks: Can be taken from anywhere inside the 'penalty area'
  • Throw-ins: Kicked in from the sideline
  • Fouls: Direct free kicks for all fouls (no indirect free kicks)
  • Yellow Cards: Player sits out for 2 minutes (team plays with 6)
  • Red Cards: Player is sent off, team plays with 6 for remainder of game

Tie-Breaking

If scores are level at full-time:

  • Straight to penalty shootout (3 penalties each)

üôè Spirit of the Game

Remember, this is a church event focused on fellowship and fun. While competition is encouraged, maintaining Christian love and sportsmanship is paramount. Aggressive behavior or arguing with referees will not be tolerated.
