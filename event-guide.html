What is SHINE?

SHINE stands for Serving Hand in Hand Impacting Needs Elsewhere. It's Cornerstone's main mission fundraising event.

This year, the SHINE Soccer Slam and Market Stalls Day is back - where you will be able to support missions, fellowship over food and play soccer together with all 8 Cornerstone congregations.

How does it work?

SHINE currency are coupons. These coupons can then be exchanged at each of the congregation stalls on the day (see below for What's On).

💰 Coupon Pricing

5 coupons for $25 or 10 coupons for $50

We encourage you to prayerfully consider how much you would like to give to missions, rather than how much you can get out of the event or what is available for sale.

And if you want to give more, why not consider gifting a coupon or two for other people to enjoy the day?

Coupons can be bought from your SHINE representative before the event, or at the coupon stall on the day, via cash or bank transfer.

Where do the funds go?

The funds raised on the day will go towards supporting Cornerstone's mission partners and projects.

If you would like to learn more, check out our missions website: cornerstonechurch.com.au/mission-initiatives

⛑️ First Aid

We pray that SHINE will be a safe event and no accidents occur. However, if you do require first aid, head to the First Aid Point (near the toilet). Our first aiders will attend to you.

🗑️ Throw it away!

Help us keep the park clean and love our neighbours by throwing away your rubbish in the bin next to each food stall.
