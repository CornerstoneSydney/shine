What is SHINE?

SHINE stands for Serving Hand in Hand Impacting Needs Elsewhere. It's Cornerstone's main mission fundraising event.

This year, the SHINE Market Stalls Day brings together all 8 Cornerstone congregations to support missions and fellowship over incredible food and activities - now with the added benefit of being indoors and protected from the weather!

How does it work?

SHINE currency are coupons. These coupons can then be exchanged at each of the congregation stalls throughout the school.

💰 Coupon Pricing

5 coupons for $25 or 10 coupons for $50

We encourage you to prayerfully consider how much you would like to give to missions, rather than how much you can get out of the event or what is available for sale.

And if you want to give more, why not consider gifting a coupon or two for other people to enjoy the day?

Coupons can be bought from your SHINE representative before the event, or at the coupon stall on the day, via cash or bank transfer.

Where do the funds go?

The funds raised on the day will go towards supporting Cornerstone's mission partners and projects.

If you would like to learn more, check out our missions website: cornerstonechurch.com.au/mission-initiatives

Sounds amazing! How do I get there?

🚗 Driving: There is parking available at the school and surrounding streets in Eastwood.

🚆 Public Transport: Eastwood Heights Public School is approximately a 10-minute walk from Eastwood Train Station. Take the T1 North Shore Line or T9 Northern Line to Eastwood Station.

🚌 Bus: Several bus routes service the Eastwood area, including routes along Rowe Street and Shaftsbury Road.

🤝 Carpooling: Consider carpooling with friends from your congregation to make the journey more enjoyable!

I'm at the event - What else should I know?

🚶 Getting around

The stalls will be set up throughout the school under different covered areas - perfect for staying dry and comfortable!

For safety, please don't go to restricted areas of the school, and supervise your own child(ren) during the event.

Follow the signs and volunteers who will help direct you to all the different stalls and activities.

⛑️ First Aid

We pray that SHINE will be a safe event and no accidents occur. However, if you do require first aid, head to the First Aid Point which will be clearly marked. Our first aiders will attend to you.

🗑️ Keep it clean!

Help us keep the school clean and show respect for the space we're using by throwing away your rubbish in the bins provided near each stall area.

🏫 Respecting the venue

We're grateful to be using Eastwood Heights Public School. Please help us leave the school in excellent condition for the students and staff.
