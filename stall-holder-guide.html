Critical Rules & Safety Requirements

üö´ NO ELECTRICITY AVAILABLE

There are NO power sockets available at the site. You cannot use any electrical appliances for cooking or operating equipment. Please plan accordingly!

üíß WATER LIMITATIONS

The two water points at the site are NOT recommended for use in food preparation or drinking. Please bring your own water for:

  • Personal hydration
  • Food preparation
  • Hand washing
  • Cleaning

üî• GAS RESTRICTIONS

IMPORTANT: Due to limited fire safety equipment onsite:

  • ‚ùå Butane gas burners are NOT allowed
  • ‚úÖ LPG burners are permitted
  • If using LPG, you MUST bring EITHER:
    • A fire blanket, OR
    • A Class C (Dry Chemical) fire extinguisher
  • Fire safety equipment must be present and easily accessible at your stall

Setup & Equipment Requirements

üè™ Tables & Shelter

  • You MUST bring your own tables - none will be provided
  • Check with your congregation elders/ministers to borrow church tables
  • Gazebo shelters are allowed but not mandatory
  • If bringing a gazebo, anchor it securely to prevent wind damage
  • Stalls will be on grass turf which may be uneven in some areas

üìç Site Access & Parking

  • Setup starts at 1:00 PM (earlier than the event start time)
  • Enter via Victoria Ave and turn left before going under the bridge
  • Limited parking near the roundabout - first come, first served
  • No reserved parking spaces for stall holders
  • Encourage your helpers to use street parking or public transport

üóëÔ∏è Waste Management

  • Each stall must set up their own rubbish bins
  • Bring adequate trash bags for the day
  • Take trash bags to the main collection point near the roundabout
  • If bins are full, please take rubbish home for disposal
  • Leave your area cleaner than you found it

Food Safety & Allergy Requirements

üç¥ Food Handling Requirements

  • All food items MUST be covered (gazebos help fulfill this)
  • Wear disposable food-grade gloves when handling food
  • Provide biodegradable serving utensils only
  • Maintain proper food temperature controls
  • Follow NSW Food Authority guidelines for temporary events

‚ö†Ô∏è Allergy Signage - MANDATORY

All food stalls MUST display an allergy sign listing ingredients. You must indicate if your food contains:

  • Peanuts
  • Tree nuts
  • Sesame
  • Soy
  • Cow's milk
  • Egg
  • Gluten
  • Seafood (Fish, Mollusc, Crustacea)
  • Artificial colours and preservatives
  • Kiwi fruit / Strawberries

Sales & Coupon System

üí∞ How Coupons Work

  • Coupons are the ONLY form of payment accepted at stalls
  • Each coupon = $5 value
  • Coupons are sold in sets of 5 ($25) or 10 ($50)
  • 10-coupon sets purchased in advance have a bonus coupon sticker on the back
  • Price your items in coupon increments (1, 2, or 3 coupons = $5, $10, or $15)

‚úçÔ∏è Marking Coupons

To redeem a coupon:

  • Mark the redemption square with any non-erasable writing instrument
  • Use: stamp, ballpoint pen, or marker
  • Fill the square completely to avoid disputes
  • Check backs of 10-sets for bonus coupon stickers
  • Keep redeemed coupons organized for reconciliation

‚è∞ Sales Timing

DO NOT start selling before 1:30 PM - We want all congregations to have equal opportunity to purchase items. The coupon sales booth will open at 1:30 PM for those who haven't pre-purchased.

üìä Expected Sales Volume

Based on pre-sales and previous years:

  • Minimum 350 expected attendees
  • $9,000+ in coupon pre-sales as of early August
  • Additional on-day sales expected
  • Plan your inventory accordingly!
