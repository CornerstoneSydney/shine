Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my coupons after purchasing?
After purchasing online, you'll collect your physical coupons from your congregation's SHINE representative. They'll be distributed in the weeks leading up to the event.
Can I buy coupons on the day of the event?
Yes, coupons will be available for purchase on the day at the information tent. However, pre-purchasing helps you avoid queues and ensures you have coupons ready to use.
What if I don't use all my coupons?
Any unused coupons still contribute to supporting our mission partners, which is a wonderful outcome. Unfortunately, coupons cannot be refunded as they represent a donation to our mission work.
Do all stalls accept coupons?
Yes, all 8 congregation-run market stalls accept coupons as payment. Each stall will have items priced at 1, 2, or 3 coupons ($5, $10, or $15).
Who is my congregation's SHINE representative?
Click the "Find Your Rep" button above to see the full list of SHINE representatives for each congregation. You can also contact the event coordinator Paul Gray at paul@paulgray.co or phone/text 0493 427 092.
