üèÜ Tournament Format & Rules

Adults Tournament (7-a-side)

  • Teams: One per congregation (8 teams total)
  • Format: Knockout tournament with consolation games
  • Match Duration: 2 x 15 minute halves + 5 minute halftime
  • Total Game Time: 35 minutes per match
  • Gender Rule: Minimum 1 female player on field at ALL times
  • Substitutions: Unlimited rolling substitutions
  • Guaranteed Games: Every team plays at least 2 matches

Kids Tournament (Ages 4-13)

  • Teams: 4 mixed teams (randomly allocated)
  • Age Range: 4-13 years old
  • Format: Round-robin for maximum participation
  • Focus: Fun, inclusion, and skill development
  • Requirement: Parent/guardian must be present
